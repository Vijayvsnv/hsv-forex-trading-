❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How are signals delivered?+
All signals are delivered instantly via Telegram and WhatsApp. You'll receive entry price, target levels (TP1 & TP2), and stop-loss with every signal — no ambiguity.
Can I upgrade my plan later?+
Yes, you can upgrade to a higher plan at any time. The remaining value of your current plan will be credited towards the new one. Contact our support team to arrange the upgrade.
Are refunds available?+
All consulting fees are non-refundable as per our policy. We strongly recommend starting with a shorter plan to test our signals before committing to a longer duration.
What is the signal accuracy rate?+
Our AI-powered system combined with CMT analyst review delivers up to 99.4% accuracy. All historical signals are documented in our public Track Sheet Records available on the website.
